Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Delicious chocolate chip cookies with flakey sea salt on top. This easy to follow recipe will change your outlook on life

Ingredients

  • Flour - 200g
  • Baking soda - 3/4 tsp
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter - 160g
  • Brown sugar - 200g
  • Refined sugar - 50g
  • Egg - 1
  • Yolks - 2
  • Vanilla extract - 2 tsp
  • Chcoolate chips or chopped chocolate, 60% at the most- 170g
  • Seasalt to finish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl
  3. Melt and brown 3/4 of butter
  4. After browning butter, melt rest of butter using the residual heat
  5. After letting the butter cool, whisk melted butter and both sugars in seperate bowl
  6. Whisk in egg, yolks and vanilla extract
  7. Fold in dry ingredients
  8. Fold in chocolate
  9. (Optional) Refrigerate cookies for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight
  10. Bake for 8-12 minutes depending on chewy you want them
  11. Finish with flakes of seasalt and let cool
