Crêpes

Description

Thin, delicious crepes rolled up up with your favorite toppings are a perfect way to start the weekend.

Ingredients

  • Refined sugar - 50g
  • Vanilla extract - 1 tsp
  • Cardamom - 1 tsp
  • Eggs - 3
  • Milk - 3 dl
  • Pinch of salt
  • Flour - 125g
  • Butter - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients, except for milk, in a bowl
  2. Put in 50-75 ml of milk, and whisk to a thick batter
  3. Whisk in rest of milk 1 dl at a time
  4. Melt butter and whisk in batter
  5. Cook until the top of the crepe is matte and edges slightly brown. Run your spatula around the perimeter, flip the crepe, and cook for another 30-60 seconds.
  6. Serve with your preffered toppings
